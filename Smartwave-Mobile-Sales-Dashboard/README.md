## 📱 SmartWave Mobile Sales Analytics & Market Intelligence Dashboard

### 1. 🎯Introduction

The smartphone industry is characterized by rapid technological advancements, intense competition, and evolving consumer preferences. Organizations operating in this domain require robust analytical tools to monitor performance and adapt strategies in real time.

### 2. 🚀Project Overview

I built an end-to-end Mobile sales market performance centralised Power BI solution designed to address a core business challenge — lack of visibility into key driver. To analyze mobile sales performance and generate actionable insights for business stakeholders. By transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights, this project demonstrates how analytics can support strategic decision-making and drive business growth.

### 3. 🎯Business Problems & Objective

**_“What drives mobile sales performance across regions, brands, and customer segments, and how can the business optimize revenue and customer experience?”_**

The organization lacked a centralized reporting system, leading to fragmented insights and intuition-based decision-making.

**Key challenges included:**

* No visibility into regional performance variations
* Poor understanding of customer behavior and demographics
* Inefficient inventory and pricing strategies
* Lack of clarity on top-performing brands and cities
* Inability to identify key revenue drivers
* No Brand wise distribution
* Inefficient targeted marketing campaigns

👉 Without clear insights, decisions were based on assumptions rather than data.

### 4. 🧩 Solution Approach:

I developed an interactive Power BI dashboard that integrates:

*	📊 Sales performance
*	🏆 Brand contribution
*	📍 City-level trends
*	👥 Customer demographics
*	💰 Pricing insights 
*	📱Product Analysis

### 5. 📂Dataset

The dataset used in this project is **SmartWave-Mobile_Sales_Dataset**.

* Contains transaction-level mobile sales data
* Includes customer demographics, product details, pricing, and payment information
* The complete dataset is available in the `/dataset` folder

### 10. 📈 Visual Analysis – Insights & Actions

 **a) KPI Cards**

 Total Quantity  : 	   19K	  =   Healthy volume
 
 Transactions	   :     4K	  =  Avg ~5 units per transaction
 
**ASP	40K	Premium-heavy market**

💡 This is not a budget market — customers are buying mid-to-high-end devices. 

👉 Strategy: Focus on value-driven, premium positioning


**b) Sales by City**

* Tier 1 (Delhi, Mumbai): Volume-driven markets
* Tier 2 (Chennai, Rajkot): Premium-focused
* Tier 3 (Gorakhpur): Price-sensitive
  
👉 Strategy: Tailor pricing & marketing city-wise


**c) 📈 Monthly Quantity Trend**

*	Fluctuations but stable demand 
*	Peaks around certain months → possible seasonal sales
  
👉 Align inventory and marketing campaigns with peak demand periods.

💡 Demand is consistent, indicating a stable market with seasonal spikes.


**d) 📅 Revenue by Day**

*	Revenue peaks on Saturdays, with noticeable mid-week dips
  
👉 Introduce weekday promotions (Tue–Thu) to balance demand.


**e)🏆 Brand Performance Table**

*	Market is highly competitive, not monopolized.
*	Apple leads with ~21% revenue share.
*	Mid-premium devices drive demand
*	Assists vendor and marketing strategy
  
👉 Strengthen partnerships with top-performing brands while expanding alternatives


**f) Customer Demographics**

*	Enable targeting the right audience
*	Target audience is mid-age working professionals
  
👉 Design marketing strategies aligned with lifestyle and income segment.


**g) Payment Behavior**

* UPI is the most preferred payment method
*	Payment methods revealed customer preference trends
*	Strong shift toward digital-first transactions.
  
👉 Leverage cashback offers and digital payment partnerships.


**h) Customer Ratings**

*	Customers around the age of 38 dominate purchases.
*	Customer satisfaction shows a positive relationship with purchasing patterns.
*	Product-market fit is strong
  
👉 Opportunity: leverage reviews in marketing


**i) Insights Ticker**

*	Key findings are summarized for quick decision-making.
*	Enhances accessibility for leadership
* Supports faster, data-driven executive decisions.
